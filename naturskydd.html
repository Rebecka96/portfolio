Redesign of Naturskyddsföreningen Halland

After one lesson of testing different plugins that can make you walk in other peoples shoes,
experience the various difficulties many people face daily when surfing the web, such as color blindness or blurred vision,
our project was to re-design the website of Naturskyddsföreningen Halland, so that it became more user friendly.
While doing the re-design we had to follow the guidelines of accessability adaptions and WCAG.
Bigger buttons, clearer contrast with pictures, texts and links, better spacing in the text, an explanatory text with every picture.
These are some of the changes that we have on our new Naturskyddsföreningen Halland.
