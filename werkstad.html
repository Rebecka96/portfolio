Creating a website for charitable work

When designing this platform, a working vaccine against the Corona virus has just launched worldwide and it's now safe to,
once again, walk the streets. But the pandemic has taken it's toll, people are scared and in more need than ever for some human compassion.
We had three weeks to prototype our design and decided to use the scrum method, and by the end of each sprint we had a day for user testing.
Based on our three user tests, we changed our profile-page because many implied that the system we had for "passions" and "skills", were confusing,
many thought that passions and skills were the same thing. We changed the naming to "good at" and "interested in" instead.
When signing in or create account we originally had a standard system and only when creating a WERK (project) you had to confirm
with BankID but many wanted to sign in with BankID from the get go instead, which we later implemented.
