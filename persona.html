The making of my persona

We got an assignment to create a persona based of our project Filmstaden x Oculus. First we had to choose
our user groups and i chose "group of friends". The next step was to decide which questions
i wanted to ask, what would simplify the persona making process. Then we sent out the questionnaire
and after the desirable amount of answers were met, i started analyzing the results and create the persona.
There were a few bumps in the road when creating Erika Johansson. When making my persona,
i noticed that several questions in my questionnaire weren't really useful and could have been replaced
by better ones that came to mind way too late.
